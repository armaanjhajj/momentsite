### what it is

**The Idea** — `strategy` — size 18
We think the internet made the world smaller but left most people feeling more alone. Moments is our attempt to do something about that. One connection (or Moment...lol) at a time. 

**Strength in Numbers** — `strategy` — size 16
We kinda realized we're not alone in feeling the way we do. There's a pretty huge and growing group of people who feel the same way about social media and want something better. If that sounds like you, you're already part of it.

**Problem** — `strategy` — size 14
One of the biggest things we've been bothered by with the current state of internet/social media is the plague of over consumption. Every app on our phones is optimized to keep your scrolling and not actually meet people. Apps like Instagram, TikTok, Twitter are primarily consumption platforms. They borrow the word “social” but that’s not really what they’re for anymore.

**Solution** — `strategy` — size 12
We’re building something that helps you find people, build a network, and show up IRL. It’s closer to the idea that Facebook was supposed to be before it became what it became, except we’re doing it with the tools and advancements we have now.

---

**Loneliness Epidemic** — `product` — size 14
More than half of young adults say they feel lonely most days. Social apps were supposed to help with this. They didn't. That's the gap we're trying to close.

---

**Relevance** — `product` — size 14
The app we're building focuses on learning about your personality and preferences to make making friends easier. We'll pay attention to what you like, where you hangout, and who you click with. The more you use it, the more relevant it gets.

**Identity** — `product` — size 16
Without giving too much away, I'm personally building a really cool way to build your identity on the app in ways I feel like current social medias don't let you. (Hopefully you won't need to spend 2 hours picking which 15 seconds of a song to put on your IG story anymore :P)

**Network** — `product` — size 16
One of the biggest goals of our app is to build a platform where you can simply connect and grow your network easily and efficiently. LinkedIn does a pretty good job at this in a professional scene but I'd like to not have to worry about someone telling me they "don't use IG" the next time i try to exchange socials. We'll help you keep track of the people you've actually met and makes your real-world circle feel like a circle again.

**Presence** — `product` — size 14
We're taking a huge (and pretty scary) step to try and use proximity to help make this social battle a tiny bit easier. Us, along with tons of others, have realized proximity is the fuel that ignites the social flame in a human. In fact, the **mere-exposure effect** describes the psychological phenomenon where people develop a preference for someone simply because they see them frequently, as familiarity naturally breeds **comfort** and **likability**. Our app will allow you to opt-in to this new movement and we'll try to help out without stepping into creepy territory. We're hoping to turn a room of strangers into a room of potential connections; only when you want it to.
